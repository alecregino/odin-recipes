How to make Halo Halo

Halo Halo is like, the dessert in the Philippines. You'll love it!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup crushed or shaved ice
  • 1/4 cup milk fresh or evaporated
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp sweetened saba or plaintain bananas
  • 1 tsp sweetened sweet potatoes
  • 1 tsp sweetened red munggo
  • 1 tsp sweetened garbanzos or beans
  • 1 tsp fresh of sweetened langka jack fruit
  • 1 tsp coconut strips or sweetened macapuno
  • 1 tsp nata de coco coconut gel
  • 1 tsp sago or tapioca pearls or jelly cut into cubes
  • 1 tsp leche flan (or more, cause that's the best part)
  • 1 tsp ube jam or 1 scoop or ube ice cream

Recipe

  1. In a tall glass, add a teaspoon of each of your selected ingredients
  2. Add sugar if you like
  3. Fill the glass with shaved ice up to the brim. Be sure to push the ice down to add more
  4. Drizzle with mlik.
  5. Add toppings of choice
  6. Serve with a long spoon and enjoy!
